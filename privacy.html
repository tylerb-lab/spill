Overview

The short version

Spill. ("we", "us", or "our") operates www.spill.life. This page explains what information we collect when you visit, how we use it, and what choices you have. We don't sell your data. We don't build profiles on you. We just write about coffee.

By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

Data collection

What we collect

We collect information in two ways:

Information you give us. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. That's it. We use it only to send you the newsletter you signed up for.

Information collected automatically. When you visit the site, standard technical data is logged — things like your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URL. This is used to understand how the site is being used and to keep it running properly.

Advertising

Google AdSense & cookies

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the web.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

Cookies used on this site may include:

  • Cookies set by Google AdSense for ad personalisation and frequency capping
  • Analytics cookies to understand site traffic and usage patterns
  • Functional cookies required for the site to operate correctly

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect how parts of the site function.

Third parties

Third-party services

We use a small number of third-party services to run the site. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Google AdSense — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP. Privacy Policy
  • Unsplash — photography used in articles. Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — site hosting. Privacy Policy

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties beyond what is described here.

Your rights

Your choices

Newsletter. You can unsubscribe from the Spill. newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising from Google at google.com/settings/ads, or via the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

Cookies. You can clear or block cookies through your browser settings. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.

If you're in the EU or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

Children

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
